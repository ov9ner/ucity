LETTER TO MY FUTURE SELF


Dear Sultan,

I hope you are well. If you are having some problems or difficulties or being stuck or have failed to do something, just remember that you can do it no matter what, and have been there before, and you overcame those problems. With that being said, let me remind you how to solve the problems that you face and help you overcome them.


HOW TO OVERCOME PROBLEMS?

First of all, try ignoring your negative thoughts such as "This is hard" or "I can't do this". Second, try resolving the problem by asking your colleagues or your mentor or by searching about it in websites such as GOOGLE and STACK OVERFLOW. If still you couldn't solve it by now, take a break and clear your mind, better yet get some sleep. After that, if you didn't solve it by now, repeat the steps again.


PROBLEM SOLVING SKILLS

Also remember that you have problem solving skills, for the reason that you have tackled some challenges and problems with building projects previously, and have learned and gained experience from them and how to deal with them. Here some of them:

  1. have the patience and the persistence it.
  2. Knowing exactly how to describe the problem.
  3. Knowing how to analyze the problem.
  4. Knowing how to search for a solution correctly.

GROWTH MINDSET:

In Establishing a Web Developer Mindset project, I learned that success is achieved by developing skills and practicing, not setting back and saying that "I have the talent for it, why should I bother? "

Also I have to hone my skills by practicing more and more and solving more problems and that will make me become good at it.

Learning from mistakes is very important, I have to see mistakes as challenges to be overcome, rather than setbacks.


GOALS:

Finally, here are the goals that I want to achieve, hopefully in the near future:

  • Benefit from this Nanodegree as much as possible.
  • Better understand the Front-End Web Development.
  • Build projects and maintain them.
  • Become a Full-Stack Web Developer.
  • Get a job as one.
